# Research Findings: Hotel Rate Shopping Market

## Market Leaders (Enterprise)

- **Lighthouse (OTA Insight)**: Real-time, parity, predictive.
- **RateGain**: Global scale, AI alerts.

## SMB Alternatives

- **SiteMinder Insights**: Ecosystem locked, snapshots.
- **RoomPriceGenie**: Automation-focused.
- **HotelPriceBot**: Lean, rules-based.

## Tech Intelligence

- **SerpApi**: Most robust for Google Hotels data.
- **Pydantic**: Critical for structured data from LLMs (as used in our Layer 2/3).

## Strategic Edge for Our Tool

- **Cost**: Pay-as-you-go via API.
- **Flexibility**: Dynamic comp-sets.
- **Clarity**: Price-to-Rating focus (ROI-centric).
